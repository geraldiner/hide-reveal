Hide & Reveal Map

This is for Codepen's challenge for the week of April 26, 2021: Hide & Reveal. It's a map that hides information about specific areas on a map. Clicking on the '+' signs will reveal some information about that area.

This is a map of my town, Happi, in Animal Crossing: New Horizons.
